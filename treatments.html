Skin lifting & tightening

Lifting & Contouring
What is it for

Designed to treat loose skin, mild laxity of the jawline and neck, and general skin sagging that occurs with age-related collagen decline.

What is the procedure

Utilizes non-invasive radiofrequency (RF) and deep tissue heating devices. Controlled heat energy is directed into the dermal layer of the skin, causing immediate tightening of collagen fibers and triggering a long-term production of new collagen and elastin.

Whom is it for

Perfect for individuals in their late 20s to 50s experiencing the early stages of skin laxity who prefer non-surgical rejuvenation with no downtime.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Completely non-invasive with zero recovery time
  • Stimulates natural collagen and elastin over time
  • Gradual, organic-looking tightening and firming of facial contours
  • Improves overall skin texture and tone density

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound)

Lifting & Contouring
What is it for

Targets sagging jowls, loss of sharp jawline definition, a double chin, and deep tissue laxity across the lower face and neck.

What is the procedure

Employs focused ultrasound waves that bypass the skin surface to deliver precise thermal energy to deep structural layers, including the SMAS (Superficial Muscular Aponeurotic System) layer. This triggers thermal coagulation zones, inducing structural contraction and powerful tissue lifting.

Whom is it for

Ideal for patients seeking dramatic jawline lifting and neck contouring without surgical scars, incisions, or extensive healing periods.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Single-session efficacy with progressive improvements over 2-3 months
  • Reaches deep tissue and SMAS layers previously accessible only via surgery
  • Leaves the outer skin layer completely undamaged
  • Long-lasting results that endure for up to a year or more

Facelift without surgery

Lifting & Contouring
What is it for

Indicated for mid-face sagging, flattening cheeks, pronounced nasolabial folds, and sagging tissue along the jawline.

What is the procedure

A customized, multi-layered suspension protocol. It combines absorbable polydioxanone (PDO) or poly-L-lactic acid (PLLA) threads to physically lift sagging fat pads, paired with structural injectables to restore lost skeletal volume.

Whom is it for

Best suited for mature clients experiencing moderate facial descent who want a visible lift but wish to avoid the medical risks, costs, and downtime of standard plastic surgery.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Immediate physical lift of loose facial skin and sagging tissues
  • Double action: physical support and long-term biological collagen production
  • Minimal downtime (typically mild swelling or bruising for 2-3 days)
  • Dramatically restores youthful proportions and three-dimensional cheek curves

Botox & Dysport

Lifting & Contouring
What is it for

Targets dynamic wrinkles caused by repetitive facial muscle movements, such as crow's feet, frown lines, forehead creases, and neck bands.

What is the procedure

Uses micro-fine needles to deliver small, highly targeted doses of purified botulinum toxin into specific hyperactive muscles. This temporarily relaxes the underlying muscle, allowing the overlying skin to smooth out completely.

Whom is it for

Adults of all skin types who want to smooth active facial wrinkles or proactively prevent deep static wrinkles from forming over time.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Quick, 15-minute in-office treatment with no recovery time
  • Visible smoothing results appear within 3-7 days
  • Preserves natural, soft facial expressions when micro-dosed precisely
  • Softens the overall appearance for a rested, refreshed look

Fillers & Facial contouring

Lifting & Contouring
What is it for

Indicated for sunken cheeks, hollow under-eye tear troughs, structural jawline recession, thin lips, and loss of chin projection.

What is the procedure

Requires precise deep-layer placement of medical hyaluronic acid or calcium hydroxylapatite fillers. These gels structurally replace lost bone volume and support fat pads, instantly sculpting and redefining facial contours.

Whom is it for

Perfect for individuals wanting to restore youthful facial fullness or enhance natural bone structure (like sharpening the jawline or projecting the chin).

Benefits of the treatment
  • Instant volume correction and anatomical lifting
  • High-definition sculpting of cheekbones, jawlines, and chins
  • Deep internal hydration as hyaluronic acid attracts water molecules
  • 100% reversible, offering a safe and customizable aesthetic solution

Salmon DNA / PDRN

Regenerative Aesthetics
What is it for

Treats dull skin, fine dehydration lines, chronic redness, atrophic acne scars, and skins struggling with compromised barrier function.

What is the procedure

Micro-injections of highly purified Polydeoxyribonucleotide (PDRN) molecules directly into the skin dermis. These DNA fragments act as building blocks, activating cell growth receptors, boosting microcirculation, and speeding up tissue healing.

Whom is it for

Excellent for patients seeking deep biological skin quality improvement, cellular repair, and a natural, healthy glow rather than purely structural lifting.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Accelerates cellular repair and tissue regeneration
  • Reduces inflammation, redness, and signs of environmental damage
  • Boosts skin elasticity and internal moisture retention
  • Improves skin health and density on a cellular level

Microneedling & MNRF

Regenerative Aesthetics
What is it for

Indicated for deep acne scars, enlarged open pores, stretch marks, fine lines, and uneven, rough skin texture.

What is the procedure

Combines mechanical microneedling with fractional radiofrequency (MNRF) energy. Sterile gold-plated microneedles penetrate the dermis to deliver targeted RF energy pulses. This controlled thermal reaction forces remodeling of old scar tissue and starts intensive new collagen synthesis.

Whom is it for

Ideal for individuals seeking to reduce the appearance of deep scars, tighten pores, and repair structural irregularities in skin texture.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Highly effective for deep, atrophic acne scars
  • Tightens open pores and improves skin texture
  • Extremely safe across all skin phototypes with minimal risk of pigment issues
  • Builds long-term skin thickness and elasticity

Pico laser

Regenerative Aesthetics
What is it for

Treats hyperpigmentation, melasma, dark sunspots, freckles, uneven skin tone, and is used for tattoo removal.

What is the procedure

Emits laser light in ultra-short picosecond (one-trillionth of a second) pulses. Rather than using heat, it creates an intense photo-acoustic wave that shatters target pigment deposits into tiny, dust-like particles. The body's immune system then naturally clears these particles.

Whom is it for

Suitable for anyone seeking correction for stubborn pigment issues, uneven skin tone, or looking to safely remove multi-colored tattoos.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Shatters stubborn pigment without causing heat damage to surrounding skin
  • Significantly lower risk of post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH)
  • Requires fewer sessions compared to older nanosecond lasers
  • Extremely short recovery time, allowing immediate return to work

Chemical peels

Regenerative Aesthetics
What is it for

Designed to address active acne breakouts, superficial dark spots, mild sun damage, and rough skin texture.

What is the procedure

Involves applying medical-grade chemical acid formulations (such as salicylic acid, glycolic acid, or TCA) to the skin. This controlled application exfoliates dead, damaged outer layers, forcing the skin to reveal fresh, healthy new cells underneath.

Whom is it for

Patients looking to dry active acne, clear clogged pores, or quickly exfoliate superficial pigment spots.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Visibly brightens and evens out skin tone
  • Reduces active acne lesions and clears congested pores
  • Smooths rough skin texture for better product absorption
  • Cost-effective skin resurfacing and renewal treatment

Acne & Acne scars

Corrective Procedures
What is it for

Indicated for persistent inflammatory acne, rolling scars, boxcar scars, and icepick scar tissue.

What is the procedure

A customized, multi-modality program. It combines subcision (physically releasing tethered scar tissue under the skin), TCA CROSS (targeted chemical reconstruction of deep icepick scars), and fractional laser resurfacing to smooth out the skin surface.

Whom is it for

Candidates dealing with legacy acne scarring or active breakouts who want a significant, structural improvement in skin smoothness.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Significant reduction in acne scar depth and visibility
  • Smooths out rough, uneven skin texture
  • Clears active acne lesions to prevent future scarring
  • Helps rebuild patient confidence in their skin

Dark circles

Corrective Procedures
What is it for

Addresses dark pigmentation under the eyes, thin skin showing blue/purple blood vessels, and shadowing caused by tear trough hollows.

What is the procedure

Utilizes a combined protocol. It uses gentle vascular and pigment lasers to clear dark pigment and restrict visible blood vessels, paired with a light hyaluronic acid filler to lift and fill tear trough shadowing.

Whom is it for

Individuals who look tired or prematurely aged due to dark, sunken under-eye circles.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Restores a well-rested, refreshed, and youthful under-eye appearance
  • Lifts tear trough hollows to eliminate shadows
  • Thickens thin under-eye skin to hide underlying blood vessels
  • Fades stubborn hyperpigmentation in the eye area

Cautery for Skintags

Corrective Procedures
What is it for

Designed to safely remove skin tags, milia, warts, and other small benign skin growths on the face and neck.

What is the procedure

Uses electrosurgical or radiofrequency cautery. Under local anesthetic, the physician applies a precision thermal tip to the base of the growth, instantly vaporizing the lesion and cauterizing the blood vessels to prevent bleeding.

Whom is it for

Anyone wishing to remove uncomfortable or cosmetically bothersome benign skin tags, warts, or milia safely in a medical clinic.

Benefits of the treatment
  • Instant, single-session removal of lesions
  • Painless procedure due to local topical anesthetic
  • Safe cauterization minimizes scarring and infection risk
  • Quick, walk-in, walk-out treatment
