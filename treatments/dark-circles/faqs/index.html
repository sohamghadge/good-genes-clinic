Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Under-Eye Dark Circles Treatment procedure take?

A standard session of Under-Eye Dark Circles Treatment at our Khar West clinic typically takes about 30 to 45 minutes of active treatment, though we suggest planning for an hour to complete consultation and preparation.

Is Under-Eye Dark Circles Treatment painful?

Most patients describe the sensation as a mild pinch or warmth. For needle-based or energy treatments, we apply a premium topical numbing cream for 30-45 minutes beforehand to ensure absolute comfort.

What is the cost of Under-Eye Dark Circles Treatment in Mumbai?

The cost for Under-Eye Dark Circles Treatment typically ranges from ₹20,000 - ₹45,000. The exact pricing depends on the surface area, severity of the condition, and number of sessions required.

Are there any side effects of Under-Eye Dark Circles Treatment?

Mild redness, slight swelling, or temporary tenderness are normal and resolve within a few hours to a couple of days. We provide comprehensive post-care guidelines to support healing.

Who is the ideal candidate for this treatment?

This procedure is ideal for patients looking to address specific cosmetic or clinical skin issues. However, Severe under-eye bags (herniated fat), chronic sinus issues, or active allergies. should be avoided.
