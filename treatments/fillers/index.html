Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the Dermal Fillers & Facial Contouring procedure take?

A standard session of Dermal Fillers & Facial Contouring at our Khar West clinic typically takes about 30 to 45 minutes of active treatment, though we suggest planning for an hour to complete consultation and preparation.

Is Dermal Fillers & Facial Contouring painful?

Most patients describe the sensation as a mild pinch or warmth. For needle-based or energy treatments, we apply a premium topical numbing cream for 30-45 minutes beforehand to ensure absolute comfort.

What is the cost of Dermal Fillers & Facial Contouring in Mumbai?

The cost for Dermal Fillers & Facial Contouring typically ranges from ₹22,000 - ₹55,000 per syringe. The exact pricing depends on the surface area, severity of the condition, and number of sessions required.

Are there any side effects of Dermal Fillers & Facial Contouring?

Mild redness, slight swelling, or temporary tenderness are normal and resolve within a few hours to a couple of days. We provide comprehensive post-care guidelines to support healing.

Who is the ideal candidate for this treatment?

This procedure is ideal for patients looking to address specific cosmetic or clinical skin issues. However, Active skin infections, history of severe allergies or anaphylaxis, or bleeding disorders. should be avoided.
