About the Doctor // Philosophy

Science-backed medical and aesthetic dermatology.

Aesthetic dermatology combines clinical expertise with personalized care. At Good Genes Clinic, we prioritize customized treatment plans designed specifically to address your long-term skin and scalp goals, rejecting high-volume, standardized models.

We operate strictly by appointment to ensure dedicated time for every consultation. This focus allows us to carry out detailed medical assessments, track treatment progress, and provide comprehensive follow-up care.

Modalities // Protocols
I.

Face Contouring

A sophisticated technique aimed at reshaping and enhancing facial structure using the Phi ratio for balanced, natural-looking, and harmonious proportions.

II.

Non-Invasive Anti-Ageing

Combat the signs of ageing like loose skin and fine lines without surgery using advanced laser therapies, HIFU, Morpheus8, and rigorous skincare routines.

III.

Liquid Facelift & Feature Restoration

Non-surgical facelifts using dermal fillers, Botox, skin boosters, and Profhilo to address sagging skin, wrinkles, and loss of volume with minimal downtime.

IV.

Youth Preservation

Natural preventative ageing treatments including PRP, PRF, and Growth Factor Concentrate (GFC) therapies to stimulate collagen production and cell regeneration.

V.

360° Hair Regrowth

Advanced therapies including QR678, GFC, PRP, and PRF to combat alopecia and thinning by stimulating the scalp with growth factors for natural hair regrowth.

VI.

Non-Invasive Body Sculpting

Cutting-edge contouring treatments including fat freezing (cryolipolysis), RF Skin Tightening, and HIFEM Sculpting to build muscle and reduce fat without surgery.

Patient Inquiries

F.A.Q.

What is the consultation process like?

+

Your initial consultation is an unhurried, comprehensive assessment of your skin architecture, concerns, and aesthetic goals. We do not rush this process. Expect a detailed discussion resulting in a bespoke protocol.

Is there downtime associated with treatments?

+

Downtime varies heavily depending on the specific modality used. Many of our non-invasive skin boosters and injectables have zero to minimal downtime, while deeper laser therapies may require a few days of social downtime.

How often should I schedule an appointment?

+

This is entirely dependent on your bespoke protocol. For youth preservation, we generally recommend quarterly visits, while active treatments like hair regrowth may require monthly sessions initially.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

+

We believe aesthetic dermatology requires precise, in-person assessment of skin quality, laxity, and structural anatomy. Therefore, we only consult in-clinic.

Consultations by appointment only.
